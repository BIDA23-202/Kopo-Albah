About Kopo Albah

Our values guide our actions in everything we do

Our Values

We Innovate. We Provide. We Care.

This is our Kopo Albah way of business.

We Innovate

Located in Gaborone, Botswana's capital, we are a mining rigging company that builds sustainable solutions for our clients. We use cutting-edge rigging technology and safety protocols to deliver comprehensive services for mining operations across Botswana.

We Provide

Our people are our strength. We provide professional development opportunities and maintain a safe work environment where our employees can excel and provide exceptional service to our clients and their mining operations.

We Care

We believe in giving back to the communities where we operate. Through our Corporate Social Investment programs, we invest in local communities and environmental initiatives across Botswana.

Core Principles

Our work, our behavior, and the way we treat each other are rooted in integrity. A positive attitude is a conscious decision – it's the key to believing obstacles can be overcome, regardless of the circumstances. We as a company and our employees are mutually committed and loyal. Honest relationships are built on open communication. A caring environment is a stable, healthy, safe and motivational environment.
